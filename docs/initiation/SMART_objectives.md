## SMART Objectives  


| Objective                            | Specific                                                         | Measurable                                                           | Achievable                                        | Relevant                                             | Time-bound            |
| ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Reduce manual report-generation time | Automate CSV/PDF exports of monthly awards data                  | Decrease staff effort from 8 h to 1.5 h/mo (80% reduction)           | Parser and export module in v1.2                  | Frees reviewer time for analysis                     | By October 31, 2025   |
| Improve audit-log completeness       | Ensure every update is captured with user, timestamp, and reason | 100% of award changes logged; < 1% missing entries                   | Implement middleware logging in v1.1              | Critical for compliance and transparency             | By August 15, 2025    |
| Increase parser accuracy             | Enhance metadata extraction from uploaded certificates           | Parser confidence ≥ 90% on ≥ 70% of uploads                          | Train model on historical sample data             | Reduces manual review workload                       | By December 1, 2025   |
| Enhance user onboarding              | Streamline employee registration workflow                        | Reduce average onboarding time from 4 days to 1 day                  | Integrate corporate email matching and reminders  | Speeds user access and reduces admin backlog         | By November 15, 2025  |
| Boost dashboard engagement           | Deploy customizable widgets and notifications                    | 70% of active users engage with ≥ 2 widgets weekly                   | Deliver core dashboard features in v1.3           | Improves visibility into award trends                | By January 31, 2026   |
| Ensure GDPR compliance               | Implement consent workflows and retention policies               | Zero consent-related audit findings; retention rules on 100% records | Build consent/retention module by v1.4            | Protects personal data and complies with regulations | By September 30, 2025 |
| Mobile & WCAG accessibility          | Deliver fully responsive UI with screen-reader support           | Pass WCAG AA audit; 100% of pages usable on smartphones/tablets      | Leverage existing accessibility framework in v1.5 | Ensures inclusive access on all devices              | By February 28, 2026  |
| Policy sandbox & rollback testing    | Validate new policy templates in an isolated environment         | Sandbox test coverage 100%; rollback tested in ≥ 3 scenarios         | Extend policy engine with sandbox in v1.6         | Reduces risk of bad policy deployments               | By December 31, 2025  |

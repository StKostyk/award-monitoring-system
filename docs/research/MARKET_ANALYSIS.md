# Market Analysis: Award Management Systems

## Executive Summary

This market analysis evaluates the business opportunity for the Award Monitoring & Tracking System within the Ukrainian educational sector. As a **solo developer portfolio project** offered as **free/open-source software**, this analysis focuses on **market validation**, **adoption potential**, and **strategic positioning** rather than traditional revenue projections.

**Key Findings:**
- **Significant Market Gap**: No transparent, GDPR-compliant solutions for Ukrainian universities
- **High Adoption Potential**: 300+ Ukrainian higher education institutions
- **Portfolio Value**: Demonstrates enterprise software development and market analysis skills
- **Scaling Opportunity**: International expansion potential in European education sector

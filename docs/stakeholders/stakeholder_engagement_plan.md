# Stakeholder Engagement Plan

## 1. Executive Summary

This engagement plan outlines the comprehensive strategy for maintaining effective communication, collaboration, and feedback mechanisms with all stakeholders throughout the Award Monitoring & Tracking System project lifecycle. The plan ensures proper alignment with business objectives while maintaining transparency and accountability.

## 2. Engagement Objectives

### Primary Objectives
- **Stakeholder Alignment:** Ensure all stakeholders understand project goals, benefits, and their role
- **Requirement Clarity:** Gather comprehensive and accurate requirements from all stakeholder groups
- **Change Management:** Facilitate smooth adoption through proactive communication and training
- **Risk Mitigation:** Identify and address concerns early through regular feedback loops
- **Quality Assurance:** Maintain high standards through continuous stakeholder input and validation

### Success Metrics
- 90%+ stakeholder satisfaction score
- 100% requirement sign-off by target dates  
- <5% scope change requests post-approval
- 95%+ user adoption rate within 3 months of deployment
- Zero critical issues from communication gaps

## 3. Stakeholder Group Strategies

### 3.1 Executive Level (Rector, Dean)

#### **Communication Strategy**
- **Approach:** High-level, business-focused, results-oriented
- **Key Messages:** ROI, strategic alignment, risk mitigation, competitive advantage
- **Tone:** Formal, executive-level, decision-focused
- **Success Indicators:** Continued funding, strategic support, policy approvals

#### **Engagement Schedule**
| Activity | Frequency | Duration | Participants | Deliverables |
|----------|-----------|----------|--------------|--------------|
| **Executive Briefing** | Monthly | 30 minutes | Rector, Dean, Dev Team | Executive dashboard, status report |
| **Strategic Review** | Quarterly | 60 minutes | All executive stakeholders | Strategic alignment assessment |
| **Milestone Presentation** | Per milestone | 45 minutes | Executive + key stakeholders | Milestone deliverable demo |
| **Budget Review** | Quarterly | 30 minutes | Rector, Dean | Budget status, forecast updates |
| **Risk Assessment** | Monthly | 20 minutes | Rector, Dean, key technical leads | Risk register updates |

#### **Communication Channels**
- **Primary:** Formal email reports with executive summary
- **Secondary:** Scheduled meetings with presentation materials
- **Emergency:** Direct phone/email to Rector's Secretary for escalation

#### **Feedback Mechanisms**
- Post-meeting feedback forms
- Quarterly stakeholder satisfaction surveys
- Open-door policy for strategic concerns
- Formal change request process

## 4. Communication Framework

### 4.1 Communication Matrix

| Stakeholder Level | Primary Channel | Frequency | Content Type | Response Time |
|------------------|------------------|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| **Executive** | Email + Meetings | Monthly | Business updates | 24 hours |
| **Management** | Email + Workshops | Bi-weekly | Operational updates | 48 hours |
| **End Users** | Email + Training | Monthly | User-focused updates | 72 hours |
| **Technical** | Documentation + Reviews | Weekly | Technical updates | 24 hours |

### 4.2 Meeting Governance

#### **Meeting Standards**
- **Agenda:** Distributed 48 hours prior
- **Minutes:** Published within 24 hours
- **Action Items:** Tracked with owners and deadlines
- **Follow-up:** Status updates provided before next meeting

#### **Meeting Types**
| Meeting Type | Purpose | Attendees | Frequency | Duration |
|--------------|---------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Steering Committee** | Strategic oversight | Executive level | Monthly | 60 min |
| **Working Groups** | Detailed collaboration | Subject matter experts | Weekly | 90 min |
| **Status Updates** | Progress communication | All stakeholders | Bi-weekly | 30 min |
| **Training Sessions** | Skill development | End users | As needed | 60-120 min |

### 4.3 Documentation Standards

#### **Document Templates**
- **Executive Reports:** Business summary, KPIs, risks, decisions needed
- **Technical Reports:** Architecture updates, security status, compliance metrics
- **User Communications:** Feature updates, training announcements, support resources
- **Meeting Minutes:** Decisions, action items, next steps, escalations

#### **Document Distribution**
- **Executive:** PDF reports via secure email
- **Management:** Detailed updates via collaboration platform
- **End Users:** User-friendly formats via multiple channels
- **Technical:** Technical documentation in shared repositories

## 5. Feedback Management System

### 5.1 Feedback Collection Methods

#### **Formal Feedback Channels**
| Method | Target Audience | Frequency | Response Time |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|---------------|
| **Quarterly Surveys** | All stakeholders | Quarterly | 2 weeks analysis |
| **Focus Groups** | End users, Management | Monthly | 1 week summary |
| **Technical Reviews** | Technical stakeholders | Weekly | 48 hours |
| **Executive Assessments** | Leadership | Monthly | 1 week action plan |

#### **Informal Feedback Channels**
- Open office hours
- Suggestion boxes (physical and digital)
- Coffee chat sessions
- Corridor conversations documentation

### 5.2 Feedback Processing Workflow

```
Feedback Collection → Categorization → Priority Assessment → Action Planning → Implementation → Communication Back
```

#### **Feedback Categories**
- **Critical:** Security, compliance, blocking issues
- **High:** Usability, workflow efficiency, feature requests
- **Medium:** Process improvements, nice-to-have features
- **Low:** Cosmetic changes, future considerations

#### **Response Standards**
- **Critical:** 24 hours acknowledgment, 72 hours action plan
- **High:** 48 hours acknowledgment, 1 week action plan
- **Medium:** 1 week acknowledgment, 2 weeks action plan
- **Low:** 2 weeks acknowledgment, future roadmap consideration

### 5.3 Feedback Tracking & Analytics

#### **Feedback Metrics**
- Response time by category
- Resolution rate by stakeholder group
- Satisfaction improvement trends
- Feature request implementation rate

#### **Feedback Dashboard**
- Real-time feedback queue status
- Stakeholder satisfaction trends
- Action item completion rates
- Communication effectiveness metrics

## 6. Change Management & Communication

### 6.1 Change Communication Strategy

#### **Change Categories**
| Change Type | Communication Method | Stakeholders | Timeline |
|-------------|---------------------|--------------|----------|
| **Strategic Changes** | Formal announcement + meetings | All stakeholders | 2 weeks notice |
| **Process Changes** | Detailed documentation + training | Affected users | 1 week notice |
| **Technical Changes** | Technical briefings | Technical stakeholders | 48 hours notice |
| **Emergency Changes** | Immediate notification | All stakeholders | Real-time |

#### **Change Approval Workflow**
1. Change request submission
2. Impact assessment
3. Stakeholder consultation
4. Approval/rejection decision
5. Communication plan execution
6. Implementation monitoring
7. Post-change review

### 6.2 Resistance Management

#### **Common Resistance Sources**
- Fear of workflow disruption
- Technology adoption concerns
- Job security worries
- Change fatigue

#### **Mitigation Strategies**
- **Education:** Comprehensive training and documentation
- **Involvement:** Include resistant stakeholders in planning
- **Support:** Extra assistance during transition periods
- **Communication:** Clear benefits articulation and success stories

## 7. Crisis Communication Protocol

### 7.1 Crisis Categories

| Crisis Level | Description | Response Time | Communication Method |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Level 1 - Critical** | System down, data breach | Immediate | All channels + emergency meeting |
| **Level 2 - High** | Major feature broken, compliance issue | 1 hour | Email + urgent meeting |
| **Level 3 - Medium** | Performance issues, minor bugs | 4 hours | Email notification |
| **Level 4 - Low** | Cosmetic issues, minor inconveniences | 24 hours | Regular update cycle |

### 7.2 Crisis Communication Team
- **Crisis Lead:** Development Team (Stefan Kostyk)
- **Business Communication:** Dean (Prof. Martynyuk)
- **Executive Communication:** Rector's Secretary (Natalia Yakubovska)
- **Technical Communication:** System Operations
- **Compliance Communication:** GDPR Officer

### 7.3 Crisis Communication Template

```
SUBJECT: [CRISIS LEVEL] - Award System Issue - [Brief Description]

IMMEDIATE IMPACT:
- What is affected
- Who is affected
- Since when

CURRENT STATUS:
- What is being done
- By whom
- Expected resolution time

STAKEHOLDER ACTIONS:
- What stakeholders should do
- What stakeholders should NOT do
- Alternative procedures (if any)

NEXT UPDATE:
- When next update will be provided
- How to get more information
- Contact for urgent questions
```
